About Our Company

ApoConsult Solutions is a leading consultation agency that provides expert human capital solutions. Our team of experienced consultants helps clients overcome challenges and achieve business goals through tailored services and long-term partnerships.

Making a Difference Together

Our support services can help organizations to manage their human resources effectively. We offer a wide range of HR services, including recruitment and placement, performance management, employee relations, skills and capacity building, and HR compliance programs. We also provide corporate support services to help organizations manage their business effectively. Our team can help setting up and implementing corporate governance frameworks, dispute, grievance and conflict resolution mechanisms, support boards of directors in running their activities and other corporate issues. We design our programs to help sustain performance of the organization, teams and individuals and enable the organization to compete effectively in today's turbulent business world. We tailor our consultancy to the specific needs of the client and through long-term relationships, ensuring there is transfer of appropriate knowledge and skills and change towards desired behavior and attitude goals.

Services Offered

  • Conflict & Dispute Resolution

    • Our expert negotiators assists organizations to navigate complex disputes and to reach mutually acceptable solutions. We work closely with all parties involved to understand their concerns / interests and find solutions that meets everyone's satisfaction.

  • Negotiations

    • We help organizations negotiate contracts, partnerships, and other business agreements. Our team has extensive experience in negotiations and can help you get the best possible deal.

  • Policy Advocacy

    • We offer policy advocacy services to help influence government policies and regulations that affect businesses. Our team assists organizations / institutions to navigate the complex policy-making process and develop effective strategies to achieve their policy goals.

  • Training and Capacity Development

    • Our training and capacity development services can help institutional teams develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed. We offer a wide range of training programs customized to organizations' specific needs.

  • Mediations and Conciliations

    • Our expert mediators and conciliators assists institutions to resolve disputes and conflicts in an amicable and respectful manner. We work closely with all parties involved to find a mutually acceptable solutions.

  • Employee & Industrial Relations

    • We offer industrial relations services to help organizations manage their relationship with their employees and unions. Our team assists in developing effective labor relations strategies, negotiate collective bargaining agreements, and manage disputes.

  • HR Support Services (recruitment, outsourcing, compliance, etc.)

    • Our HR support services assists organizations to manage their human resources effectively. We offer a wide range of HR services including, organizational reviews, local and international recruitment and selection; labor outsourcing and management, performance management, establishment of HR processes, HR compliance & social audits, HR policies and procedures i.e disciplinary, grievance and workplace grievance investigations, contracts of employment, employee separation i.e redundancies, terminations & dismissal advisory support, exit interviews, career counseling, mentorship and coaching.

  • Compliance & Certification Support

    • Assist Businesses and Organisations in their Voluntary Sustainability Standards (VSS), compliance and certification journey, through pre-audits, compliance training, documentation and preparedness.
